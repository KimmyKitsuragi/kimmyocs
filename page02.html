A mysterious entity that seemed to have manifested outside of the Somnium after Birgit's first exorcism in the Kingdom of Castavelli.

Its true form is only perceivable by Birgit and Sofia. To others, it adopts the appearance of a human, influenced by what the perceiver thinks they should be in that context. It uses its weapon, the Dream Nail to anchor itself to reality.

Appears to be allied with Sofia and are aligned in their goals to assassinate Birgit for unknown reasons.
